## 🏥 MedLingua: Bridging the Gap Between Doctor and Patient

Nearly 2 in 3 patients struggle to understand their healthcare providers due to complex medical jargon and disconnected patient data. MedLingua addresses this problem by synthesizing structured health records and unstructured clinical notes into clear explanations, personalized recommendations, and meaningful visualizations.

---

### 📖 Overview
MedLingua is a healthcare data interpretation platform designed to improve communication between patients and providers. By combining structured medical records with unstructured clinical notes, the system generates easy-to-understand explanations while preserving clinical accuracy. This empowers patients with clarity and supports providers with connected data insights.

---

### 🌟 Features
- **Personalized Explanations**: Converts complex medical terminology into patient-friendly language.  
- **Smart Recommendations**: Provides tailored healthcare recommendations based on individual patient data.  
- **Medical Visualizations**: Displays connected medical histories through intuitive charts.  
- **Structured & Unstructured Data Integration**: Merges EHR data with clinical notes seamlessly.  
- **Provider-Focused Insights**: Supports clinical decision-making with organized data views.  

---

### 📝 Usage
- Upload or connect structured patient medical data.  
- Submit unstructured clinical notes for processing.  
- View automatically generated explanations in simple language.  
- Receive personalized health recommendations.  
- Analyze patient history through interactive medical visualizations.  

---

### 🛠️ Technologies Used
- **Backend**: FastAPI  
- **Frontend**: SvelteKit  
- **Machine Learning**: Codebox  
- **Natural Language Processing (NLP)**: Custom NLP Models  
- **Database**: SQL  
- **Data Processing**: MIMIC Dataset & Clinical Data Pipelines
